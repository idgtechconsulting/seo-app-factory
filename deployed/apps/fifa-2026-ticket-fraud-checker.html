    Common Scam Patterns

    🚩 Payment Method

    Asking for payment via WhatsApp, Telegram, Crypto, Zelle, or Friends & Family (PayPal). These offer zero buyer protection.

    🚩 URL Red Flags

    URLs ending in .xyz, .top, .biz, .info or containing words like "cheap", "discount", or "ticket-sale".

    🚩 Urgency Tactics

    "Only 2 tickets left!", "Price increases in 1 hour!", or "Direct from official source but must act now!"

    🚩 Too Good To Be True

    Finals tickets sold at face value or below during peak demand. If the price is suspiciously low, it is a scam.
