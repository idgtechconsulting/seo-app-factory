Theorycrafting Guide

When deciding between stats, look at the Average DPS. In many RPGs, adding a flat bonus to base attack is more valuable early on, while percentage modifiers scale better in the late game.

Crit Tip: If your Crit Multiplier is low (e.g., 150%), increasing your Attack Power is usually better than increasing Crit Rate. Once your multiplier hits 250%+, Crit Rate becomes your most powerful scaling factor.
