Understanding Your STR Metrics

Occupancy Rate is the percentage of your available nights that are booked. High occupancy is great, but if it's 100%, you might be underpricing your property.
Average Daily Rate (ADR) is the average income per paid night. To calculate this, we divide total revenue by the number of booked nights.
